Metro Feeder Service Now Available at GoodEarth Malhar!

By GoodEarth  |  Published On: February 9, 2024
Image courtesy: https://www.newindianexpress.com/

We’re thrilled to announce the launch of a full-fledged Metro Feeder service in GoodEarth Malhar by Bengaluru Metropolitan Transport Corporation (BMTC). The MF-46 bus route, directly links GoodEarth Malhar to Challaghatta Metro station. This collaborative effort between our community and the State’s transport department represents a significant milestone in enhancing accessibility and connectivity.

No longer do residents need to rely solely on individual vehicles.

Instead, they can enjoy the convenience of public transportation, reducing their carbon footprint and easing congestion on our roads.

Join us on the journey

Metro Feeder Route No. MF 46 will provide seamless connectivity from Kengeri Bus Terminal, Kengeri Metro, Orchard Road, Doddabele Village, Doddabele Junction, VBHC Palmhaven, Provident Sunworth, Benedictine School, and finally to Challaghatta Metro Station.

The MF 46, easily recognizable by its vibrant orange color, will operate in a loop, making 21 trips a day in the specified direction, including non-peak hours.

Residents and visitors alike can rely on this convenient service for their daily commute or leisurely travels, with a travel time of just 12-15 minutes from Kengeri Bus Terminal to GoodEarth Malhar, the designated GEM Bus Stop located at the Petrichor Organic Store within our community.

This development represents a significant enhancement in accessibility for our community, providing a sustainable transportation option that aligns with our values of eco-conscious living.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to all stakeholders involved in making this initiative a reality.

We encourage all residents and visitors to take advantage of this enhanced Metro Feeder service, further enriching your experience at GoodEarth Malhar.

Disclaimer

Please be advised that the metro feeder bus service may be subject to interruptions or discontinuation by BMTC at any time for various reasons beyond our control. GoodEarth assumes no liability for any inconvenience or consequences resulting from such interruptions.
