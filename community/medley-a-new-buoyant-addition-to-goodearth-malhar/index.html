Medley which was started in 2018 braved great uncertainties unleashed by the pandemic, and yet it is today the sixth energetic community of GoodEarth Malhar.

A True Melange of Housing Options

Medley is uniquely designed to integrate four distinct architectural layouts into a single, cohesive neighborhood landscape:

🏡
Townhouses
Intimate vertical living layouts designed for modern families.
🏢
Villaments
Perfect fusion of standalone villa spaciousness and apartment ease.
🌇
Walk-ups
Terraced units offering active direct access to the outdoors.
🏰
Low-rise Flats
Spacious apartment levels blending seamlessly with surrounding flora.
Built on four acres, comprising four types of houses and 119 units, Malhar Medley is a place where one would love to create memories that last forever.
