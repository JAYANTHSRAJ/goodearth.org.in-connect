A melding of designs

The entrance of Medley, acting as a threshold of the community, is an amalgamation of various design elements creating a captivating and inviting space. As you step into this area, you are greeted by a vibrant garden with oxide-finished seating and a tranquil pond, setting the stage for the unique pavilion that stands as a portico to the entire community. The pavilion features stacked stone pillars and playful elements embedded with seating, transforming it into an active, usable space where children can play and residents gather. Amidst these eye-catching features, an unassuming element holds a deeper meaning and showcases the meticulous craftsmanship that went into its creation – the reinforced concrete ceiling. Though it may seem modest at first glance, it is a testament to the intricate workmanship and thoughtful design embedded in every corner of Medley.
