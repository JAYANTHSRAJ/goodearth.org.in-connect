Co-founder and Principal Architect, Jeeth Iype in a free-wheeling interview talks about the beginnings of GoodEarth in Bangalore. From building residences and smaller communities to scaling up, resulting in the creation of GoodEarth Malhar Eco-village, Kengeri, spread across 50 acres with 600 families living in a thriving environment.

“Having created sustainable communities for more than 35 years, GoodEarth has paved the way for others to emulate and build vibrant eco-systems to live, work and play,” says Mr Iype.
