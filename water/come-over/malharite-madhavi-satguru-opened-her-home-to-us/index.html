Come over | Meet Madhavi Satguru and Family

By GoodEarth | Published On: March 7, 2025

Madhavi Satguru followed in the footsteps of her elder sister and moved into their forever home at Malhar two years ago. A few days ago, she, her husband Prabhu, and their daughter Khushi opened up their home to us and shared more about their life and routines. From the musical weekends to the snack and study sessions to the rhythms of Madhavi’s mom’s sewing machine, everything about their home reflects their love for the simple pleasures of life spent with family and friends in the lap of nature.
